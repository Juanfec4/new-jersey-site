New Jersey

Welcome to New Jersey

Located in the northeastern part of the United States, New Jersey offers a mix of beautiful beaches along the Atlantic Ocean and charming countryside. Home to landmarks like Ellis Island and Newark's cityscape, the state is rich in history and diverse cultures. From the lively atmosphere of Atlantic City to the peacefulness of historic sites like Princeton, New Jersey invites visitors to enjoy a blend of nature and culture, making it a compelling destination for a memorable experience. Fun facts

  1. First Boardwalk The world's first boardwalk was built in Atlantic City in 1870, marking the beginning of a popular seaside tradition that has spread to many coastal destinations.

  2. Historical Innovation Hub Thomas Edison's laboratory, where he invented the phonograph and the first practical electric light bulb, is located in Menlo Park, New Jersey.

  3. Diner Capital New Jersey is often referred to as the "Diner Capital of the World," boasting a high concentration of diners serving a variety of delicious comfort foods 24/7.
